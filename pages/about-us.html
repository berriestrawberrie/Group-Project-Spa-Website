Do you ship internationally?

Of course! Salford.com ships internationally to 150+ countries. Our products is also available for purchase at select Sephora stores throughout Europe. For a full list of all international retailers, please visit our store locator page..

Are your products vegan, cruelty-free and environmental friendly?

Our products are cruelty-free and never test on animals, nor do we allow suppliers to conduct animal testing on our behalf. While many of the products are free of animal byproducts, Salford is not considered a vegan line: A handful of our products may contain ingredients of animal origin, such as beeswax and carmine. For information on specific products, please refer to the Ingredients tab on the product pages. Green & Earth is fully vegan line. We also try to have a low impact on the environment.

Do you sell gift vouchers?

Yes. These can be for specific treatments or a monetary amount. Vouchers can be purchased through this website, over the telephone or in person at the spa and are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. Gift cards once purchased may not be refunded, except in accordance with your online purchase legal rights and is not exchangeable for any form of cash or credit. Cancellations for booked services received with 24 hours notice will be issued with a full refund. Booking and pre-payment is essential for all treatments and thermal spa experience sessions.

How do I contact customer service?

You can either send us an email at info@salford.com or by messaging us through our social media platform (see below).
