When should I arrive for my treatment?

We recommend that you arrive about 10 minutes before your treatment so you can relax and rehydrate. The therapist will pick you up when it is time for treatment.

Are your products vegan, cruelty-free and environmental friendly?

The products we use are cruelty-free and never tested on animals, nor do we allow suppliers to conduct animal testing on our behalf. While many of the products are free of animal byproducts, Salford is not considered a vegan line: A handful of our products may contain ingredients of animal origin, such as beeswax and carmine. Green & Earth is fully vegan line. We also try to have a low impact on the environment.

Do you sell gift vouchers?

Yes. These can be for specific treatments or a monetary amount. Vouchers can be purchased through this website, over the telephone or in person at the spa and are valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. Gift cards once purchased may not be refunded, except in accordance with your online purchase legal rights and is not exchangeable for any form of cash or credit. Cancellations for booked services received with 24 hours notice will be issued with a full refund. Booking and pre-payment is essential for all treatments and thermal spa experience sessions.

How do I contact customer service?

You can either send us an email at info@salford.com, call or messaging us through our social media platforms.
